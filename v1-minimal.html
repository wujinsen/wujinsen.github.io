About

From Java 6 to Project Loom. I've shipped on every major release.

Based in Tokyo since 2018, I lead backend teams designing high-throughput payment and order systems for Japan and Southeast Asia. Before that I built recommendation engines and large-scale data pipelines at a Shanghai-based unicorn.

I obsess over the quiet things that matter in production: clean domain boundaries, observability, graceful degradation, and writing the kind of code a stranger can confidently change at 3 a.m. I also publish a weekly newsletter on distributed systems that has 2,400+ subscribers.
